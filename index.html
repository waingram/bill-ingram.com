Bill Ingram is a Research Programmer for Scholarly Communication and Repository Services in the University Library at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He leads the group of library application developers responsible for analyzing, designing, implementing, and supporting software systems related to scholarly communications and digital libraries. He is technical lead for various projects and services supporting scholarly communications at the library, including the Illinois Digital Environment for Access to Learning and Scholarship (IDEALS) and the Electronic Thesis and Dissertation deposit service. Prior to this, Ingram brought his programming skills combined with his in-depth knowledge of metadata, digital library development, and user services to bear on several research grant projects.
